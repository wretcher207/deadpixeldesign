About

Most digital agencies were built to win awards from other digital agencies. We were built for something else.

"We don't optimize. We haunt."

Dead Pixel Design is a full-service digital shop for people who believe a website should feel like something when you land on it — not just load fast and convert. For businesses that want to exist on the internet with a little weight behind them.

We handle web design, social media, chatbots, and digital strategy. Small teams. Real work. No account managers explaining your brand back to you.

The founder has spent fifteen years making music under the name Wretcher — self-produced, self-recorded, every layer written and engineered by hand. He also builds his own software tools from scratch when the ones that exist aren't good enough. That's not a personality detail. That's the reason this work doesn't look like everyone else's. When you've spent a decade and a half learning every layer of a craft because there's no one else to ask — it changes how you think about what it actually takes to make something that holds.

David W. Russell III
Founder & Creative Director — Dead Pixel Design

How We Work
01
We Show Up With Something

Before the first conversation, we do the homework. Depending on what we know about your business and your industry, we arrive with a working proof of concept already built — not a mood board, not a proposal deck. Something you can click through and react to on the spot.

02
We Listen Before We Build

What have you tried that didn't work? What does success actually look like for you — not in marketing language, in real terms? What are your time and budget constraints? We ask the questions most designers skip, because the answers change everything about what gets built.

03
We Move Fast and Stay Close

Once we're aligned, we build quickly. You'll hear from us throughout — not with status updates that waste your time, but with decisions that need your input. We're communicative without being needy. You stay informed without babysitting the project.

04
We Get It Right

The first delivery is a starting point, not a finished product. We present, you respond, we adjust with surgical precision. No arguing about vision. No scope creep. Just the work, refined until it's exactly what it needs to be — then handed off clean.
